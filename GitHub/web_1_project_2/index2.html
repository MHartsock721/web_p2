Spokane Sequential Submission

My biggest goal as an animator is to be able to create characters and stories that people can connect with and grow to love. In addition to working on animations and character designs in my free time, I have submitted a short comic to the artist magazine known as 'Spokane Sequential.' In November of 2018, it was published in the one year anniversarry issue. It's the first time I have been able to professionally show my work and hope to have more doors open for me, as an artist, along with this opportunity
